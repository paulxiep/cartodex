# Cartodex Corporate Contributor License Agreement

Based on the Harmony Agreements 1.0 (CLA-E) template, adapted as a
*License Grant* (not Copyright Assignment) for the Cartodex project. Use
this variant when a contribution is made on behalf of a corporation
(typically because the contributor created the work in the scope of their
employment).

---

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Cartodex ("**We**" or "**Us**").

This contributor agreement ("**Agreement**") documents the rights
granted by contributors to Us. To make this document effective, please
sign it and send it to Us by electronic submission, following the
instructions in [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md). This is a legally
binding document, so please read it carefully before agreeing to it.
The Agreement may cover more than one software project managed by Us.

## 1. Definitions

**"You"** means the entity which Submits a Contribution to Us.

**"Designated Employees"** means the employees of You designated in
Section 9 below or such other employees designated by means of any
written notice from You to Us in the future. You may amend the list of
Designated Employees at any time by written notice to Us.

**"Contribution"** means any work of authorship that is Submitted by
any of Your Designated Employees on Your behalf to Us in which You own
or assert ownership of the Copyright.

**"Copyright"** means all rights protecting works of authorship owned
or controlled by You, including copyright, moral and neighboring
rights, as appropriate, for the full term of their existence including
any extensions by You.

**"Material"** means the work of authorship which is made available by
Us to third parties. When this Agreement covers more than one software
project, the Material means the work of authorship to which the
Contribution was Submitted. After You Submit the Contribution, it may
be included in the Material.

**"Submit"** means any form of electronic, verbal, or written
communication sent to Us or our representatives, including but not
limited to electronic mailing lists, source code control systems, and
issue tracking systems that are managed by, or on behalf of, Us for
the purpose of discussing and improving the Material, but excluding
communication that is conspicuously marked or otherwise designated in
writing by You as "Not a Contribution."

**"Submission Date"** means the date on which a Designated Employee
Submits a Contribution to Us on Your behalf.

**"Effective Date"** means the date You execute this Agreement or the
date the first Contribution is Submitted by a Designated Employee on
Your behalf, whichever is earlier.

### 2.3 Outbound License

Your Contribution in a Material, We may license the Contribution under
any license, including copyleft, permissive, commercial, or
proprietary licenses. As a condition on the exercise of this right, We
agree to also license the Contribution under the terms of the license
or licenses which We are using for the Material on the Submission Date.

**Note**: this clause is the relicense-enabling clause. It explicitly
permits Cartodex to relicense Your Contribution under terms different
from the license in effect when Your Designated Employee Submitted,
while preserving the license that was in effect on the Submission Date
as a floor.

either direct or indirect.

You acknowledge that We are not obligated to use Your Contribution as
part of the Material and may decide to include any Contribution We
consider appropriate.

### 2.6 Reservation of Rights

Any rights not expressly licensed under this section are expressly
reserved by You.

under this Agreement.
